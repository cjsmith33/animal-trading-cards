English Bulldog

Bulldogs were officially inducted into the American Kennel Club in 1886.

  • Life expectancy:: 8 to 10 years
  • Weight: Female: 40–51 lbs, Male: 51–55 lbs
  • Temperament: Willful, Docile, Friendly, Gregarious
  • Colors: White, Fawn, Piebald, Brindle & White, Fawn & White, Red & White, Red Brindle, Red

The Bulldog, also known as the British Bulldog or English Bulldog, is a medium-sized dog breed. It is a muscular, hefty dog with a wrinkled face and a distinctive pushed-in nose.
